Crisis Support Lines

If you're experiencing a mental health crisis or having thoughts of suicide, please reach out for professional help immediately:

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988 or 1-800-273-8255

Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

Find A Therapist

Working with a licensed mental health professional can provide personalized support for your journey:

- Psychology Today Directory

- BetterHelp (Online Therapy)

- Talkspace (Online Therapy)

Self-Help Books

- "Feeling Good" by David D. Burns

- "Self-Compassion" by Kristin Neff
